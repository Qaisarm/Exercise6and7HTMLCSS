Summary:

Soldier Ray Garrison has recently been killed in combat but has been given back his life - now as the superhuman Bloodshot. With an army of nanotechnology in his veins, he is an unstoppable force - stronger than ever and with an ability to self-heal directly. But the company that resurrected him, the RST corporation, controls not only his body but also his brain and his memories. Ray Garrison doesn't know what's real and not - but he's going to find out.
